Some stuff about me.

Hey there, people. I'm Emma LaPolt! I'm a 3rd year AGPM major at UCSC. I'm studying games because I love indie, queer, artsy video games, and I want to be part of queer game history! I also have a strong interest in accessibility in games. In fact, I switched my major from theater to games after taking a class on accessible games, and to this day it's my favorite class I've ever taken. I also love cartoons, YouTube, and scary movies (okay, maybe not too scary). :p
