Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

The purpose of this lab was to learn the process of creating pseudocode through real life activities.

Challenge

We have decided to make a pseudocode for streaming music. We listed the things a music player, such as Spotify, would do when we, as user, pick a song to play. We also considered what tasks it will implement with settings decided beforehand,such as shuffle and loop.

Problems

There were little to no problems during this lab, other than getting teletype to work!

Results

Good stuff. We made two lists of pseudocode, one for solitaire and one for streaming music. We then updated our work on here!
